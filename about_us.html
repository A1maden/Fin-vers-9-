What We Believe

A company without a philosophy is like a ship without a compass. Our agency is built on the premise that it is possible to make a creative playground rather than a production factory. The equation is simple: happy people create joyful digital experiences.

We are lucky because we have 3 offices with many creative brains from different cultures that we smash together in a process called neural fusion (in contrast to neural fission). Who We Are

According to legend, he or she who can transform Monday into the best day of the week will be awarded with endless street credibility, balloons and a real sense of accomplishment. So we took the challenge and opened Hello Monday in 2006 in Aarhus, Denmark.

The publicity was huge, people could not believe that Mondays could actually be great days. We, of course, did not want to be greedy with our winnings so we decided to spread the goodness and opened 2 more offices, one in Copenhagen and one in New York City. 3 offices, 36 Mondayteers, 2 thriving plants, and 1 dying plant. We are not sure what killed it.
